Pantalan | Gingoog City, Philippines



Kwek-Kwek


Kwek kwek is a popular Filipino street food made from eggs that are coated in orange-colored batter and then deep-fried. It's a popular and affordable snack in the Philippines, enjoyed by many for its crunchy exterior and soft, flavorful interior. This Kwek-Kwek is the best Kwek-Kwek I've ever tasted.

Ice Cream Cookies


An ice cream with the flavor of cookies stands out. The cookies I've tasted in it are unique compared to all the ice cream flavors I've tried before. This one leaves a yummier aftertaste, and it doesn't melt easily. I'm really fond of it; that's why it's a popular choice among many.

Siomai


Siomai is a type of Chinese dumpling popular in many Asian countries. It typically consists of a seasoned ground meat mixture, wrapped in a thin dough wrapper, and then steamed. This siomai is really affordable, with three pieces for only 10 pesos. It has a unique taste; it's not like any other type of taste, and it is quite small, but worth it for the price. However, I must admit, I don't like it that much.
